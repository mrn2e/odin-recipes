Recipe for Apple Pie

Description

Just like a good pumpkin pie or pecan pie, we all need an easy to make apple pie recipe. Whether you want to serve a classic holiday dessert for your Thanksgiving or Christmas gathering or, you're just in the mood for the irresistible taste of cinnamon and apples, I guarantee you've found yours here!

Ingredients

  • White Sugar
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Ground Nutmeg
  • Lemon Zest
  • Sliced Apples
  • Lemon Juice
  • Pie Crust
  • Milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Mix sugar, flour, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, and lemon peel
  3. Line a 9-inch pie pan with the crust, layering apples in 1/3 at a time, sprinkling mixture between layers
  4. Place second pie crust on top and flute edges. Cut vent holes at the top
  5. Bake for 40-50 minutes
  6. Allow to rest before serving
