Recipe for Miso Soup

Description

This miso soup is full of savory flavors. Add more dashi to your soup if you want a stronger stock. You can use yellow, white, or red miso paste for this soup â€” yellow miso is sweet and creamy, red miso is stronger and saltier.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dashi Grounds
  • Miso Paste
  • Diced Silken Tofu
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine and boil water and Dashi Granuels
  3. Reduce to medium heat and stir in miso paste
  4. Stir in Tofu
  5. Allow to rest before serving with green onions
